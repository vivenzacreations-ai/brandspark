About The Artist

Gaurang Pithiya

Established in May 2022, Mr & Mrs Resin Art began as a passionate work-from-home endeavor and has grown into a premier destination for floral preservation and resin craft in Ahmedabad.

We specialize in turning wedding Varmalas into everlasting keepsakes like clocks, frames, and blocks. Beyond creating art, we support the community as a trusted dealer of raw materials including epoxy resin, hardeners, and pigments.
