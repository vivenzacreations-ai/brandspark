Frequently Asked Questions

How does the WhatsApp booking process work?
Simply tap the "Book via WhatsApp" button on any deal or custom request. It opens a pre-filled message. Our agents will instantly reply with availability, payment links (UPI/Bank Transfer), and issue your e-ticket directly to your phone.
Are the flash deal prices guaranteed?
Airfares fluctuate constantly. The prices shown on our flash deal board are highly accurate as of the daily update. However, to secure the exact price, immediate booking via WhatsApp is required before the airline modifies the inventory.
Do you provide corporate or group booking discounts?
Yes, as a Private Limited company, we specialize in corporate travel management and bulk group bookings (including Hajj/Umrah groups). Please reach out via WhatsApp or email for dedicated B2B rates.
