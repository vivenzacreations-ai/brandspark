About Unico Arts

Master Craftsmanship

Based in Saharanpur, India, Unico Resin Art specializes in fusing nature's wood with modern epoxy resin techniques. We are a premier manufacturer and exporter dedicated to creating timeless pieces.

From bespoke dining tables to intricate wall clocks, every item is a unique masterpiece, customized to your specific desires and built to export-quality standards.
